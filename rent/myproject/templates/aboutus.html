About us

Kantipur Realestate is one of the best online platform for those who want to buy, sell and rent their properties.

It is a well formed organization which is established with a vision to market the real estate in Nepal and to represent builders and buyers in an organized, transparent and bring in professional integrity in Nepalese real estate business. Kantipur Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. offers a one-step solution for every realty business. It first understands your needs and then advices and finds the perfect property within your budget. In addition, it also facilitates a transparent buyer-seller agreement to ensure smooth transfer of property within shortest period of time. Starting from Kathmandu and with proposed showrooms in the various part of Nepal, they are committed in the promotion and marketing of properties from across the country so that the prospective buyers and sellers have one window that provides comprehensive details about every new real estate project in the country. By infusing intelligence and innovation into the way people search for property, find a buyer, tenant or locate an expert, they create a unique property experience for all customers. Offering clients easier ways to discover lucrative real estate opportunities, they have aim to be Nepal’s one of the most recognized and respected brands for property; operating successful businesses ranging from residential and commercial property to property management, short-term rentals and property.
We connect buyers and sellers. We always make sure that the property which we are marketing here has clear titles, good quality construction and right location for respective purposes. This is the best platform for our clients and partners in their journey of discovering, renting, buying, selling the properties. We advertise your property in our website. We have normal listing plans and featured listing plans. If you want to post your property as featured, which is shown in the homepage, we charge applicable fees. Always feel free to contact us.
